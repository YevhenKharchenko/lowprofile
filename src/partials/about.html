Behind every image, there’s a life unfolding

At first, it feels like a simple puzzle. But then you notice something — the scenes are connected.

  • The characters return. The places evolve. What looked like a single image becomes part of something larger.

  • A quiet café in one puzzle becomes a meeting place in another.
    A child painting alone later shares their world with someone else.

These aren’t just pictures.
They’re moments from stories you piece together yourself.
And the more you play, the more those stories begin to stay with you.
