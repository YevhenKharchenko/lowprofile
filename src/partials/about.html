Every thread tells part of the story

So each level starts with these threads that look like someone's cat got into a yarn basket - total chaos everywhere.

But nothing here is random.

The more you look, the more you begin to see patterns. Paths that almost work. Lines that nearly align. Small decisions that created the mess you now have to untangle.

Your task is simple to explain, but never trivial to complete:separate what is crossed, restore what is clear.

And as the puzzles grow, so does the complexity of what you’re seeing. Not just more threads — but more possibilities.
